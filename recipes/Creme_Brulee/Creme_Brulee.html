Creamy Creme Brulee Recipe

Here is a classic Crème Brûlée with a silky, smooth and rich custard topped with a thin pane of crunchy toffee.

Ingredients

  • Cream
  • Vanilla Bean
  • Sugar
  • Egg Yolks

Steps

  1. Scrape the vanilla bean.
  2. Infuse the cream with vanilla
  3. Make egg mixture
  4. Add vanilla cream to the egg mixture
  5. Make custard
  6. Fill ramekins
  7. Water bath
  8. Bake 35-40 minutes
